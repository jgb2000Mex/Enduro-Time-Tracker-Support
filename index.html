Basic Usage

Setting Up a Race

  1. Open the app on your Apple Watch
  2. Select "Timetable" from the main menu
  3. Configure Parc Ferm√© if needed
  4. Add your time controls with their scheduled times
  5. Press "Done" when finished

During the Race

  1. Select "Race Timer" from the main menu
  2. The app will display the time remaining until the next control
  3. You'll receive automatic alerts at 2 minutes, 1 minute, and exact time
  4. Use the lock button to prevent accidental touches
  5. If you need to apply a penalty, use the penalty button
Important note: Make sure you have HealthKit and Location permissions configured if you want to record your races as workouts with GPS.
